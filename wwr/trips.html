Our Trips

1 Day Boaz River

Enjoy one action packed day full of excitement. This trip is great for anyone in need of an adventure no matter the skill level or group size. You will encounter thrilling drops and lots of white water.

2 Day Rocky Canyon

This trip includes an overnight stay in the canyon. At the end of an exhausting day of rafting join us with a restful evening around the campfire and then a night under the stars. The second day will be filled with exploring caves in the canyon along with rafting!

3 Day Ultimate Passage

The Ultimate Passage is not for the faint of heart. With canyoneering included in this rafting trip, one must be in great shape and have experience climbing.
